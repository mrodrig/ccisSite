  • Interesting Facts about me...

    At the age of two I had already mastered the concept of using a computer mouse. My first desktop computer ran Windows 95. In my lifetime so far, I have had 3 desktop computers and 5 laptops. Needless to say, I am constantly updating to the latest and greatest technology. At the age of twelve, I built my first desktop computer. It had an Intel Core 2 Duo E6300 processor. The stock speed of 1.86GHz however did not seem fast enough to me. After many trials, I finally decided on a very stable overclock at 3.01GHz. With that said, I like to push the boundaries of technology and think outside of the “clock.”
